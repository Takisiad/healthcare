Welcome to HealthAI

Empowering Health with AI Innovation

At HealthAI, we are a university-driven startup revolutionizing healthcare through ethical AI. Our mission is to make medical guidance accessible, safe, and inclusive, connecting users with professional services while prioritizing privacy and trust.

Our Story

HealthAI is a university-born startup created by four computer science students specializing in Systems Integration and Intelligent Systems. We provide ethical medical guidance via AI bots, emphasizing safety, privacy (HIPAA/GDPR compliance), and inclusivity.

Our Services

AI Chatbot

Evidence-based medical advice, doctor recommendations, wound analysis, dietary and sport programs, SOS features, medication verification, and recovery programs.

Pharmacy Services

Home delivery of medications, prescription-only access, AI verification systems, and comprehensive order tracking for your convenience.

Home Nursing & Visits

Professional home nursing services with easy scheduling and ambulance safety protocols for emergency situations.

Medication Reminders

Automated notification system to help you stay on track with your medication schedule and dosage requirements.

Doctor Portal

Comprehensive profile management, medical databases access, and secure client communication platform for healthcare professionals.

Special Needs Support

Inclusive interfaces and specialized AI designed to support users with special needs and accessibility requirements.

Emergency Integration

Real-time emergency calling system integrated with local emergency services for immediate medical assistance.

Reservations & Analysis

Easy appointment scheduling system with receipt and medical document analysis capabilities for comprehensive care.
